### About me

I'm a student developer working at the intersection of learning and real-world software. I design and build full-cycle mobile apps with **Flutter + Firebase** — from idea and architecture to UI and deployment. Sensitive logic always lives on the server, never on the client.

As an **AI Engineer**, I integrate LLM models (Gemini) into real products and design the prompts behind them. I also design product UI/UX in Figma. My approach is simple: **prototype first, build second.**

### What I'm building

- **NEXGEN UA** — a national school hackathon I founded. I'm both the organizer and the sole developer of the whole platform (landing, dashboards, admin panel, auth, RLS).
- **A+ Power** — a school rewards & currency app (Flutter, Firebase, Cloud Functions).
- **AIK Pass** — a digital school-pass security system with server-side QR validation.
- **AudioPlace** — an audio-guide app: full product & UI/UX design in Figma.
